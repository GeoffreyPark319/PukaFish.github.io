About This Project

Genshin Impact is a popular game created by Hoyoverse, formally known as Mihoyo, on September 28, 2020. This game was made available on mobile, Playstation, and PC and has made over three billion dollars worldwide. The game follows the story of universe-traveling twins who were separated in the world of Teyvat by an unknown goddess. There, we follow one twin’s journey to find their sibling by traveling to each country and talking with the archons who know all. They are accompanied by friends and enemies as they try to solve the true nature of this world.
This game is well known for the vast amount of characters that range from really popular to acknowledged existence. Each character has a unique skillset and design which holds up well in battle with the right builds. Players can aim for these characters, both meta or the favorited, characters by “wishing”, a gacha mechanism, in game. This site observes and compares a character’s popularity based on two sites: Reddit and Google.
This site hopes to answer these two questions: Who is the most popular character based on the opinions of Reddit and Google. Who is the least popular character on the same platforms?

Data Collection

The first set of data, Reddit data, is collected from over 400 posts in the all time most popular (upvoted) posts. Popularity is gathered by seeing when a character first appears in the form and how often they appear in the posts after that.
The second set of data, Google data, is collected from observing Google Trends and how much search results a character got. While the data can be inaccurate due to the many ways you can search up a character, this data use the keywords: “[Character] Genshin”.
